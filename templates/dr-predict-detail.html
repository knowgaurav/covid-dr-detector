Detect Diabetic Retinopathy

Project Brief:

Diabetic Retinopathy is an eye complication that arises due to diabetes. It caused when the blood vessels of the retina (light-sensitive tissue) get damaged and vision gets affected. Uncontrolled sugar level is the major cause as it damages the blood vessels and makes them swell & leak. In the majority of the cases, there are no early symptoms and usual symptoms start to show when there is vision loss or considerable retina damage. It is estimated that up to 80 percent of diabetic patients who have had diabetes for more than 10 years are most vulnerable to diabetic retinopathy.

Diagnosis of Diabetic Retinopathy is highly dependent on manual extraction and trained medical professionals are needed for detection, management and treatment of the disease. With the lower availability of trained professionals in developing nations, it has become a daunting task for the patients to get treated and save their vision. Therefore, in this project, we try to build a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to automatically diagnosis the disease at its different stages from retinal fundus images.
